    A FOOTBALL LIFE JOURNEY

    FROM A ROSARIO BEACH
    TO THE WORLD CUP

    Inspired by Lionel Messi — the little boy from Argentina who kicked off a legend.

    scroll to begin ↓
    01

    The Kickoff

    Every great career starts with a single kickoff — the first touch, the first dream. Ours begins on the sandy riverbanks of Rosario, Argentina, where a small boy with a worn-out ball played barefoot against the wind off the Paraná.

    No academy, no stadium. Just sand, a wall, and a thousand touches a day. That is where a footballer is really made — long before the lights.

    02

    The Club

    Talent finds its way. The boy leaves home and crosses an ocean to FC Barcelona, the cathedral of football. Under the Camp Nou lights he learns that touch, vision and heart beat raw power. This is the grind — the years of training that turn a promise into a phenomenon.

    Messi made his Barcelona first-team debut in 2004, aged 17, and went on to become the club's all-time top scorer.

    03

    The World Cup Finals

    The peak. The FIFA World Cup — the loudest stage in sport, where a nation holds its breath. Here the journey comes full circle: the barefoot kid from Rosario, now wearing the number 10, chasing the one trophy that had always escaped him.

    In 2022, at last, the boy from Rosario lifted the World Cup — completing football's greatest career arc.

    Your Kickoff

    Kickoff Rush is a tribute to that road: beach, club, World Cup — three arenas, one life. It is fiction, but the feeling is real. Every time you press play, you are that kid again, taking the first touch of a brand-new journey.
